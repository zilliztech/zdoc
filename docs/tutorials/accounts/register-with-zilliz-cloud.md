# Register with Zilliz Cloud

In this guide, you will learn how to create an account for Zilliz Cloud services.

## Before you start{#before-you-start}

Open the signup page at [https://cloud.zilliz.com/signup](https://cloud.zilliz.com/signup).

![NDfrbpRGYoK4sOxpLCecRC3Ankb](/img/NDfrbpRGYoK4sOxpLCecRC3Ankb.png)

## Procedure{#procedure}

Currently, there are several options available to register an account with Zilliz Cloud:

- With a work email and password

- Linking to a Google Account

- Linking to a GitHub Account

### With work email and password{#with-work-email-and-password}

To register for a Zilliz Cloud account with your work email and password, follow these steps:

1. Enter your work email address in the **Work Email** field.

1. Enter a password in the **Password** field.
    Your password should be no more than 128 characters and must include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character.

1. Check the box next to **I agree to the [Terms of Service](https://zilliz.com/terms-and-conditions) and [Privacy Policy**](https://zilliz.com/privacy-policy).
    We recommend that you read our **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy** before proceeding.

1. Click **Continue**.
    You will receive a confirmation code at the email address you provided.

1. Enter the confirmation code in the prompted dialog box and click **Verify**.
    If you haven’t received a confirmation code, click **Resend Code** to try again.

:::info Notes

Upon your first login using your verified credentials, we kindly invite you to provide information about yourself and your workload. This will help us better understand your needs and serve you more effectively.

:::

### Linking to Google account{#linking-to-google-account}

To link your Google account with Zilliz Cloud, click the button with the Google logo to start the process.

Before proceeding to the next step, you need to select the checkbox next to **I agree to the [Terms of Service](https://zilliz.com/terms-and-conditions) and [Privacy Policy**](https://zilliz.com/privacy-policy), and then click **Submit**.

Once you have been redirected to [https://accounts.google.com](https://accounts.google.com), enter the email address or telephone number registered with Google. Then, enter the password corresponding to the provided email address. Once you are all set, you will be redirected back to Zilliz Cloud with a success toast.

### Linking to GitHub account{#linking-to-github-account}

To link your GitHub account with us, click the button with the GitHub logo to start the process.

Before proceeding to the next step, you need to select the checkbox next to **I agree to the [Terms of Service](https://zilliz.com/terms-and-conditions) and [Privacy Policy**](https://zilliz.com/privacy-policy), and then click **Submit**.

Once you are redirected to [https://github.com/login](https://github.com/login), enter your username or email address and the corresponding password registered with GitHub. Once you have completed the process, you will be redirected back to Zilliz Cloud with a success toast.

:::info Notes

Before linking your GitHub account, make sure that you have already verified your email address with GitHub and made it public. Otherwise, the registration process may fail. Refer to the related FAQs for more information.

:::
